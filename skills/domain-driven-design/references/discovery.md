# Discovery

Extract raw domain material before designing.

Look for:

- domain terms
- actors and roles
- commands
- business decisions
- domain events
- policies
- invariants
- external systems
- pain points
- open questions

Useful output:

| Element | Candidate | Evidence | Open Question |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |

Do not normalize language too early. Preserve competing terms until domain
experts resolve them.
